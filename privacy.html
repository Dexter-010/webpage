Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

DEXTERITY.IT (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you interact with our website, services, and communications.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (contact forms, event registrations, training enrolments) and technical data captured automatically (IP address, browser type, pages visited). Cookies and analytics tools help us improve the experience.

2. How We Use Information

Personal data is used to respond to enquiries, deliver contracted services, improve offerings, and share relevant updates. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

We may share information with trusted partners who support service delivery (e.g., hosting, billing). All partners are bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.

4. Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined or comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion where allowed by law.

5. Your Rights

You have rights to access, correct, restrict, or delete personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights or to withdraw consent for marketing communications.

6. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect data. No method of transmission is 100% secure; please contact us if you suspect unauthorized activity.

7. International Transfers

When data is processed outside your jurisdiction we ensure appropriate safeguards such as contractual clauses and regional hosting controls.

8. Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy enquiries email privacy@dexterity.it.
